About Multiple App

Multiple App allows you to clone and run multiple accounts of your favorite Android apps on a single device. Manage personal and work accounts separately without switching devices.

Key Features

✔ App Cloning
Create unlimited copies of supported applications.
✔ Dual Accounts
Use two or more accounts simultaneously.
✔ Privacy Protection
Keep cloned apps isolated and secure.
✔ Lightweight & Fast
Optimized performance with low memory usage.
